Our Story

Bridget Hailey began her love of chocolate in 1985 when she got her first job at a local confectionary. That time, she realised how enthusiastic she was about baking so 5 years later she decided to leave that place and start her own business. At first, her parents were not approving of that; they kept reminding her that it would be really difficult especially at the beginning, until she would attract her customers to visit her confectionary instead of other ones more 'popular'. But Bridget was desperate enough so to start her own business calling it 'Sweet Temptations'. It did not take long enough for people to start spreading rumours around about how tasty and delicious her cakes are, so very soon her confectionary had become very popular. More and more people daily would visit the store to try theirselves Bridget`s recipes. Nowadays, Sweet Temptations counts 5 successful stores, located around the city and several thousands of fully satisfied customers.
